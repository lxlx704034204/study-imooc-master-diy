Freemarker内建函数

1. 字符串内建函数

    [#list "a|b|c|d"?split("|") as item]
  • ${item}
    • [/#list] [#assign var1 = "01/03/2017"?date("MM/dd/yyyy")] [#assign var2 = "23:45:30"?time("HH:mm:ss")] [#assign var3 = "2016-12-31 03:05 PM"?datetime("yyyy-MM-dd hh:mm")]
  • ${var1}
  • ${var2}
  • ${var3}

2. 数字类型内建函数

    [#assign numVar1 = 314.5662/]
  • ${numVar1?string("0.##")}
  • ${numVar1?round}

3. list内建函数

    [#assign listVar1 = [1,2,3,4, 11,12,13,14, 21,22,23,24] /]
  • ${listVar1?chunk(4)?size}
    • [#list listVar1?chunk(4)?last as item]
  • ${item}
    • [/#list]

4. 其他内建函数

    [#assign sVar1 = 'hello' /]
  • ${sVar1?is_number?string('yes','no')}
  • ${sVar1?has_content?string('yes','no')}
  • ${sVar2!"ss"}
  • ${("1+2")?eval}
  • ${"1"+"2"?is_string?string('yes','no')}
