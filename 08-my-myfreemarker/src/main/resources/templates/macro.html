macro demo

1. macro：宏指令

  • 例子1：无参数的macro
    • [#macro test1] 我是无参数的macro [/#macro] [@test1 /]
  • 例子2：有参数的macro
    • [#macro test2 param1 param2] 我是有参数的macro,param1 = ${param1},param2=${param2} [/#macro] [@test2 param1="java" param2="python"/]
  • 例子3：有默认参数的macro
    • [#macro test3 param1 param2="python"] 我是有参数的macro,param1 = ${param1},param2=${param2} [/#macro] [@test3 param1="java" param2="hello python"/]
  • 例子4：有多个参数的macro
    • [#macro test4 param1 param2="python" paramExt...] 我是有参数的macro,param1 = ${param1},param2=${param2}
    ${paramExt['param3']} [/#macro] [@test4 param1="java" param2="hello python" param3="javascript" param4="nodejs"/]

2. nested

  • 定义
    • [#macro test1 param1="java"] ${param1}
    [#nested param1,"我的nested参数"]
    [/#macro]
  • 调用
    • [@test1 param1="java";loopVar1,loopVar2] hello ${loopVar1},${loopVar2}
    [/@test1] [@test1 param1="python";loopVar1] hello ${loopVar1}
    [/@test1]

3. 函数

  • 定义
    • [#function doAdd param1 param2] [#return param1+param2] [/#function]
  • 调用
    • 你好，我是调用${doAdd(100,100)}
